About

I am an MUEP student at Arizona State University and Graduate Teaching Assistant in the School of Geographical Sciences and Urban Planning, Tempe, Arizona.

I completed my Bachelor of Urban and Regional Planning at KUET, Bangladesh, graduating 2nd of 69 students with a CGPA of 3.83/4.0 and two consecutive Dean's Awards.

My work spans air quality modeling, aerosol retrievals, remote sensing, wildfire emissions, ecosystem services, flood modeling, and community-scale environmental health.
